Who are we?

SPARSH, a techno cultural club saw it’s inception in the year 2014. Formed by the then Computer Science and Engineering students of Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering now known as, JSS Science and Technology University, SPARSH was found to be hugely appreciated by students for the club’s activities and it’s well known “SPARSH talks”. SPARSH caters to students' desires and invites their role models to our campus. All kinds of special people ranging from singer, sportsperson, writer, actor etc., to scientist, engineer, CEO etc., are invited, all of whom have a past to share, success to celebrate, profound talents to showcase or life lessons to teach. Through SPARSH talks, students have met, talked and expressed their ideas, beliefs or fear in realising their dreams, while learning a lot from what the big people had to say about their life, struggles and many things more.
